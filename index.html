When will you get your first rewards?
From the day you start delegating to us, we will start paying your first rewards after approx. 36 days (12 cycles).
It takes 7 cycles for the Tezos blockchain to confirm the delegation, and 5 more cycles to unfreeze the first rewards.
Keep also in mind that we do not pay rewards to any accounts found with zero balance.

How safe is it to delegate to us?
It is absolutely safe. You will not lose any of your Tezos.
You only give us the permission to bake your coins on your behalf.
You retain a full control on your balance.
